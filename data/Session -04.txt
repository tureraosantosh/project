-> Software compaines can be categorized into 3 types
            1)Product Based compaines.
            2)Service Based compaines.
            3)OutSourcing compaines.

->The compaines which will develop Product and selling to the people
in the market is called as Product Based compaines.

-> For Product Based compaines clients will not be available they will have only customers
                    ex. microsoft,HP,IBM,Google etc

-> In Product Based compaines constarate on below concepts.
        1)Data Structure.
        2)Algorithm.
        3)Design patern.
        4)System Design.
        5)Problem solving.
Note : In Product based compaines conduct coding test, using hackrank and hackearth.
